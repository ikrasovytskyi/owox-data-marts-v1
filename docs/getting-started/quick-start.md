| Deployment Option | Guide | Best For |
|-------------------|-------|----------|
| **Local Deployment** | [Local Deployment Guide](./deployment-guide/local-deployment.md) | Quick testing, learning, or running a demo on your own computer (macOS/Windows/Linux). |
